Welcome to The Green Solution!

Each year, farmers apply herbicide to their crops to eliminate common weeds. Yet, excess herbicide application leads to increased amounts of glyphosate in groundwater, which can wreak havok on the environment.

With our Green Solution Calculator, farmers can reduce their usage of harmful herbicides such as RoundUp by only targetting affected areas. Our calculator automatically calculates the amount of herbicide you need, and provides you with a simple cost analysis so you can see how much you'll save.
